To add a chart to your JSF page, include the following HTML fragments into your code:

Markup:

<!-- page.xhtml -->
<html lang="en" xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1999/xhtml"
	xmlns:wc="http://googlecode.com/wickedcharts">
	<body>
		<wc:chart id="chart1" options="" theme="" />
		<wc:chart id="chart2" options="" themeUrlRef="" />
		<wc:chart id="chart3" options="" themeUrlRef="js/myTheme.js" />
		<wc:chart id="chart4" options="" themeUrlRef="http://js.mySite.com/myTheme.js" />
	</body>
</html>
